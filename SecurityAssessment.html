Network Security Risk Assesment (SRA)

The Importance!

Within your network setup, there must have been a security assessment done, and measures have also been set to prevent events that can harm the continuity of your business or service. For instance, in your hardware setup , there are UPS devices placed to prevent power outages from harming your equipment. There is a firewall to prevent easy access from unauthorized people with most likely harmful intentions.
Regarding the use of the network, you have determined whether social channels are allowed, etc.

Risk assessments involve the process of making choices for the implementation of measures to lower risks. This can be as follows:

  1. The avoidance of certain activities to decrease
  2. Accepting the risk (with the consideration that it might have certain benefits)
  3. Terminate the risk

So, what you can expect from us is:

  • ✮Risk Identification
  • ✮Risk Analysis
  • ✮Risk Evaluation
  • ✮Risk Mitigation
