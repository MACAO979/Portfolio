The Trajectery To MAC Services

.Started as a Customer Service Agent, offering 2nd line helpdesk assistance to customers of Telfort mobile network users.
Afterwards she started at a broadband cable company (UPC Netherlands) offering tripple play services, there she had the task of ensuring that SLA.s of business accounts were met and securing redundancy of their connection. By having close contact with the national operation centre(NOC) which monitores the SDH and VHF netwrok, she gained various knowledge of network monitoring and handiling.

Besides these experiences she had the oppertunity to be the General Secretary of Telecommunications Authoriting body which oversees the implementation of new technologies and the legislation there of. This experience has landed her great knowloged regarding permit handling, ETSI and FCC type approval, lisencsiing in Telecommunications and IT in general.

Furthemore in 2014 she became certified Communications Officer.

Since she always had a passion for technolgie in 2026 she started a study in System and Network Management. For the subject Web Technologies she had the assignment to create portfolio and this extended in starting her own company where her obtained knowledge of administrtaion, communiction and IT skills during her studies are combied to over solutions in these areas to companies.
