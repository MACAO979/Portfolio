Communication Management

As Communication is key in life, so it also is in business. Now days the internet is a big part of how and where we communicate. An online presence is must for a business. MAC Services is here to assist companies in getting that online presence and more.

Within communication Management we have two main distictions internal and external.

How can and will we assist you

At MAC Services, we first make an assessment of what your current lines and methods of communication are, and then create a communication plan that can harbor both internal and external communication. As we offer a full communication service, therefore, depending on your needs and wishes, this can be extended with a marketing plan.

To whom do you want to communicate

Internal or external

Both the communication with your employees or staff and with your customers or future customers are equally important. It must be handled with the utmost care and tact. For internal communication depending on your wishes, need and the scale of your company, we may set up:

  1. A newsletter on a monthly basis where you share company updates.
    • a video message from the CEO, CFO, or other chiefs in operation can share certain information.
    • employee feedback.
    • Department news , For example, a sales department could share their numbers of sales for that month or information regarding new customers.
    • updates on new products or services.
  2. Build an intranet where employees can log in and access certain information they do not use daily but need to know or use in the line of work. Furthermore, with different sections containing social activities and quizzes, that give them the oppertunity to engage more with the company.
  3. A standard circular letterhead with different templates that you can customize to address specific matter in a formal manner.
These are just some examples of the different solutions we can offer for internal communication.
