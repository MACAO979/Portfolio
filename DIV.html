What Clasiisfication fit your Ducoments

Internal or external use

To have a process that fits your business, it is important to classify your documents. Based on the classifications, there will be parameters set for each department regarding how to handle and who is authorized to handle the document. Due to preset unauthorized access being tackled , your process is better equipped to deal with AVG and privacy issues. Since each lookup is recorded within the software, it is always traceable who accessed which document and when.

Storage

Same as your analog archive it is import how you storage your digital documents. considartion point are the cost, will you choose a cloud option or ohisical servers, on location or at a data center. Further depending on the control you which or need to have on who can acces the storge.

Automated Classification processor

With the digitalization

For most documents, it is clear to what class they belong . It gets a little more challenging when you have documents that are in between classes or do not have a class that clearly defines them.
For this, we use Automated Classification, making use of algorithms and machine learning to detect text in the document that can be assigned to a certain label.

As this is very helpful in saving time and reducing the use of resources. In certain cases, a manual classification check is still needed to make sure that documents are classified correctly.

In your company, your designated DIV Manager or Officer will also have the task to frequently check documents and their automated assigned classification
Clasiisfication, Handling and Acces

Storage also important in Digitalizatiion

As you can see in our permit request, there are 5 visible lanes. In those lanes, you have different blocks that have certain tasks. These blocks represent departments or job titles.

Depending on the classification of the document at the time of handling, a department or person with a certain job title gets access.
The 6th lane, which is not visible, is reserved for the administrator and other people within the company who have executive rights, such as the:
*❇️Director
*❇️General Manager
*❇️CEO.

Note that these accesses are also logged and are traceable ; deletions are kept in storage. The time of storage depends on the software.
